abstract: "The FAIR data principles — Findability, Accessibility, Interoperability and Reusability — have become a cornerstone of open science policy. Yet implementing FAIR in large-scale, federated storage environments remains challenging. This paper describes the metadata subsystem of Onedata, which supports both schema-free JSON metadata and RDF-based linked-data annotations. We present the Onedata Metadata Graph, its integration with persistent identifier services (Handle, DOI), and the OpenAPI-compatible REST endpoint that exposes rich dataset descriptions to data catalogues. Case studies from three European research infrastructures demonstrate how automated metadata harvesting accelerates dataset discovery across provider boundaries."
